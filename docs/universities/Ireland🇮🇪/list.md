# Ireland Uni List

## 1. Trinity College Dublin (圣三一大学)

**Overview:**
Trinity College Dublin (officially *Trinity College, the University of Dublin*) is Ireland’s oldest and most prestigious university, founded in **1592** and located in the heart of Dublin. It combines a long academic tradition with strong research and global engagement. The university offers a wide range of programs across arts, sciences, engineering, health, and humanities, with rich cultural and student life on its historic campus. It is renowned for its excellent faculty, extensive library (including the famous Book of Kells), and vibrant international community.

**QS World University Rankings:**

-   **QS 2026:** Ranked around **75th in the world** and **1st in Ireland**.
-   **QS 2025:** Previously ranked **87th globally**.

Trinity performs especially well in many **subject rankings**, with several disciplines like Nursing, English, Pharmacy, and Performing Arts placed in the global top 50.

## 2. University College Dublin (都柏林大学 / UCD)

**Overview:**
University College Dublin (UCD) is one of Ireland’s largest and most globally engaged research universities. Situated on a spacious campus in Dublin, UCD offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fields such as arts and humanities, business, engineering, science, health and agricultural sciences, and social sciences. It attracts a large international student body and emphasizes research, innovation, and employability. UCD also has strong industry links and high graduate outcome rates.

**QS World University Rankings:**

-   **QS 2026:** Ranked approximately **118th globally**.
-   **QS 2025:** Ranked **126th globally** (showing an upward trend).

UCD is known for its broad subject strengths and global research partnerships, and it often scores well in categories like sustainability and internationalization.
