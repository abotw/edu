# UK Uni List

### 1. **The University of Edinburgh**

A leading research-intensive university in Scotland, founded in 1583. Known for strong programs across sciences, humanities, and social sciences, it has a vibrant international community and historic campus in Scotland’s capital.
**QS Ranking:** ~27 (2025) / ~34 (2026) globally.

### 2. **The University of Manchester**

A large and prestigious university in England’s northwest, known for world-class research, engineering, business, and science programs. It has notable achievements in research impact and innovation.
**QS Ranking:** ~34 (2025) / ~35 (2026) globally.

### 3. **King’s College London (KCL)**

A major London university with strengths in law, humanities, health sciences, and social sciences. Its central London location and professional connections make it popular for disciplines like medicine and international relations.
**QS Ranking:** ~40 (2025) / ~31 (2026) globally.

### 4. **University of Southampton**

A respected research university in the south of England, known for engineering, oceanography, computer science, and business. It is active in research partnerships and has a strong international student body.
**QS Ranking:** ~80 (2025) / ~87 (2026) globally.

### 5. **University of Birmingham**

A Russell Group research university in central England with strong programs in sciences, arts, business, and health. It emphasizes impactful research and offers a broad student experience.
**QS Ranking:** ~80 (2025) / ~76 (2026) globally.

### 6. **University of Leeds**

One of the UK’s largest universities, well-regarded for its research and teaching in business, engineering, social sciences, and arts. It combines a vibrant student life with strong industry links.
**QS Ranking:** ~82 (2025) / ~86 (2026) globally.

### 7. **University of Glasgow**

A historic Scottish university with strengths in medicine, science, engineering, and humanities. It balances long academic tradition with modern research and global engagement.
**QS Ranking:** ~78 (2025) / ~79 (2026) globally.

### 8. **Durham University**

A collegiate university in northeast England, known for excellent teaching quality, strong student satisfaction, and rigorous academics in arts, sciences, and social sciences. Its historic setting enriches the student experience.
**QS Ranking:** ~89 (2025) / (~within top 100) (2026).

### 9. **University of Sheffield**

A research-led university in South Yorkshire, with strong faculties in engineering, sciences, and social sciences. It is known for interdisciplinary research and a supportive campus community.
**QS Ranking:** ~105 (2025) / ~92 (2026) globally.

### 10. **University of Nottingham**

A major university with a broad range of programs, strong research output, and international campuses. It is known for quality in sciences, engineering, business, and arts.
**QS Ranking:** ~108 (2025) / ~97 (2026) globally.

------

**Notes on QS Rankings:**

-   QS World University Rankings evaluate institutions on academic reputation, employer reputation, citations per faculty, faculty/student ratios, and internationalization.
-   Rankings can shift slightly each year due to changes in methodology and performance.
